📖 Aircraft Overview

The Boeing 787 Dreamliner introduced a new generation of commercial aviation by using carbon-fibre reinforced composites throughout much of its structure. This reduced weight while increasing efficiency, allowing airlines to operate longer routes with lower fuel consumption and improved passenger comfort.
