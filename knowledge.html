Boeing 787 Dreamliner

The Boeing 787 Dreamliner is among the world's most advanced wide-body commercial aircraft. It combines lightweight carbon-fiber composites, highly efficient engines, digital avionics and advanced aerodynamics to provide better fuel efficiency and passenger comfort.

Composite Material

Overview

The Dreamliner introduced one of the largest technological advancements in commercial aviation by replacing aluminium with composite materials, reducing weight while increasing strength and efficiency.

Interesting Facts

✔ First commercial aircraft with extensive composite fuselage.
✔ Uses electric systems instead of traditional pneumatic systems.
✔ Wings flex dramatically during flight.
✔ Lower cabin altitude improves passenger comfort.
✔ Reduced fuel consumption by approximately 20%.
✔ Operated by more than 80 airlines worldwide.
