✈ Boeing 787 Dreamliner

The Boeing 787 Dreamliner is one of the world's most advanced commercial aircraft. It was designed using lightweight composite materials, highly efficient turbofan engines, and advanced aerodynamic technology to improve fuel efficiency and passenger comfort.

Specifications

  • Manufacturer : Boeing
  • Type : Wide-body Airliner
  • Range : 14,140 km
  • Cruise Speed : 903 km/h
  • Passengers : 242–335
  • First Flight : 2009

Interesting Facts

• Around 50% of the aircraft is made from carbon-fiber composites.

• Larger windows improve passenger comfort.

• Lower cabin altitude reduces passenger fatigue.

• Advanced wing design improves lift and fuel efficiency.
