✈ Boeing 787 Dreamliner

The Boeing 787 Dreamliner is among the world's most advanced commercial aircraft. It combines lightweight composite materials, fuel-efficient turbofan engines, advanced aerodynamics and digital avionics to improve performance, passenger comfort and operating efficiency.

Specifications

Range

14,140 km

Cruise Speed

903 km/h

Passengers

242–335

Manufacturer

Boeing

Overview

The Dreamliner introduced extensive use of carbon-fiber composite structures, larger passenger windows, lower cabin altitude, improved humidity systems, and modern fly-by-wire technology.

Interesting Facts

  • ✔ Around 50% composite materials.
  • ✔ Larger electronically dimmable windows.
  • ✔ Lower cabin altitude reduces passenger fatigue.
  • ✔ High fuel efficiency compared to previous generations.
  • ✔ Advanced wing design improves lift and efficiency.
