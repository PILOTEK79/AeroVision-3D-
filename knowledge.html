Boeing 787 Dreamliner

The Boeing 787 Dreamliner is one of the world's most advanced passenger aircraft. It uses lightweight composite materials, efficient engines and modern avionics to improve performance and passenger comfort.

Range

14,140 km

Speed

903 km/h

Passengers

242–335

Manufacturer

Boeing

Overview

The Dreamliner introduced carbon-fiber composite structures, larger dimmable windows, lower cabin altitude and improved fuel efficiency, making it one of Boeing's most successful long-haul aircraft.

Interesting Facts

✔ Around 50% composite materials.
✔ Fly-by-wire flight controls.
✔ Advanced wing flex technology.
✔ Lower cabin altitude for comfort.
✔ Improved fuel efficiency.
✔ Used by airlines worldwide.
