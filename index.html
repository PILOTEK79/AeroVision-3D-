📖 Aircraft Overview

The Boeing 787 Dreamliner introduced a revolutionary approach to aircraft design by replacing much of the aluminium structure with lightweight carbon-fibre reinforced composites. This innovation improves fuel efficiency, increases range and provides passengers with a quieter and more comfortable cabin experience.

💡 Interesting Facts

First commercial aircraft with a mostly composite fuselage.
Consumes roughly 20% less fuel than previous generation aircraft.
Its wings flex dramatically during turbulence.
The cabin altitude improves passenger comfort.
Uses electric systems instead of traditional pneumatic systems.
Operated by airlines across the world.
